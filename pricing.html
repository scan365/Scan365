Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all Pro plan purchases. If you are not satisfied within 14 days, contact admin@itsl.com.au for a full refund.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings or by emailing admin@itsl.com.au. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal and AfterPay. All payments are processed securely by Paddle, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment provider.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes. IT Service Link supports Australian non-profit organisations and educational institutions with special pricing. Contact admin@itsl.com.au with your organisation details to enquire.

Is GST included in the prices?

All prices shown are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and exclusive of GST. A tax invoice including GST will be provided for all purchases by IT Service Link (ABN 78 336 526 604).

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Contact admin@itsl.com.au to discuss plan changes.
